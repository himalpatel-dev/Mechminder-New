# MechMinder Onboarding Content

This file contains the suggested text and structure for the "Startup / Onboarding" screens of your application. You can use this copy to populate your intro slides.

## Slide 1: Welcome & Overview
**Headline**: Master Your Maintenance
**Subtext**: Welcome to MechMinder. The distinct way to track your vehicle's health, history, and handy documents all in one place.
**Visual Idea**: An illustration of a happy person standing next to a clean, shiny car.

---

## Slide 2: Service History
**Headline**: Never Miss a Service
**Subtext**: Keep a detailed digital log of every repair, oil change, and part replacement. Know exactly what was done and when.
**Visual Idea**: A wrench or checklist icon representing maintenance entries.

---

## Slide 3: Smart Reminders
**Headline**: Stay Road Legal & Safe
**Subtext**: Get timely alerts for insurance renewals, PUC expiry, and upcoming maintenance. We remember dates so you don't have to.
**Visual Idea**: A bell or calendar icon with a notification badge.

---

## Slide 4: Expense Management
**Headline**: Track Every Penny
**Subtext**: Monitor fuel efficiency and service costs with beautiful charts. See exactly where your money goes.
**Visual Idea**: A pie chart or bar graph showing cost analysis.

---

## Slide 5: Digital Glovebox
**Headline**: Papers in Your Pocket
**Subtext**: Securely store digital copies of your License, RC, and Insurance. Access them instantly whenever you need them.
**Visual Idea**: A folder or document scanner icon.

---
